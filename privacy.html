Anonymous Analytics, Cookies and Tracking

shaya.click uses Simple Analytics to understand aggregate website usage, such as page views, referrers, approximate location, device type and browser category. This analytics setup is intended to measure traffic patterns, not identify individual visitors.

Anonymous analytics can show patterns. It cannot identify named visitors or prove that a specific person visited this website.
